Health Trends in USA, China, and India

The project aims to communicate trends in health and life expectancy over time, healthcare expenditure by country, and mortality rates by cause.

Original life expectancy dataset: https://data.worldbank.org/indicator/SP.DYN.LE00.IN

Original healthcare expenditure dataset: https://data.worldbank.org/indicator/SH.XPD.CHEX.GD.ZS

Original mortality rates dataset: https://www.who.int/data/gho/data/themes/mortality-and-global-health-estimates
